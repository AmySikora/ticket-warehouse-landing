Why duplicate exposure matters

One overlap can become a costly mess

When the same seats appear in more than one place, the result can be broken fulfillment, refunds, support escalations, chargebacks, and public complaints.

Manual review breaks down fast

Brokers often end up comparing sections, rows, seats, and listing exports by hand. That takes time, creates inconsistency, and makes easy misses more likely.

Earlier visibility changes outcomes

You do not need a giant platform to improve review. Better visibility into listing overlap gives teams a practical way to catch risk sooner and act with more confidence.
