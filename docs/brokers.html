This page focuses on the broker-side use case: reviewing exported listings, spotting overlap, and identifying patterns worth manual follow-up.

Why this matters

Duplicate exposure creates risk

The same seats showing up in more than one place can create confusion, broken fulfillment, seller disputes, refunds, and lost trust.

Manual review is slow

Brokers often end up comparing rows, sections, and seats by hand across exports, screenshots, or marketplace views. That is tedious and easy to miss.

Early visibility helps

You do not need a giant enterprise platform to start improving review. Better visibility at the event level already helps teams act sooner.

How broker review works

  1. Upload or review exported listing data for an event
  2. Compare sections, rows, and seats across listings
  3. Flag possible duplicate exposure or overlap
  4. Review the findings and decide what needs follow-up
