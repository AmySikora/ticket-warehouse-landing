Marketing Plan

The goal is simple. Help people learn what Ticket Warehouse does. Find early users who want to stop selling the same ticket twice. Share real results and clear stories.

What Ticket Warehouse Does

It keeps ticket listings in sync across every site. If a ticket sells on one site, all the other sites update right away. That prevents a second sale of the same seat. It saves time and avoids refunds.

Who We Are Talking To

  • Independent ticket sellers who list on more than one site
  • Venues and promoters who want clean and accurate listings
  • Marketplaces that want to protect buyers and trust

Plan by Phase

Phase 1. Months 1 to 2. Launch the site. Share the story. Talk with small brokers. Post short and clear explainers.

Phase 2. Months 3 to 4. Publish examples and simple guides. Share early results. Improve the product with feedback.

Phase 3. Months 5 to 6. Reach more marketplaces and venues. Share success stories. Look for partners and investors.

What We Need

We plan to raise $25,000 for hosting, outreach, legal setup, and development time.

Our Message

One ticket. One sale. One source of truth. Clear, fair, and simple for everyone who sells tickets online.
