Why duplicate exposure matters

Seller confusion

The same seats appearing in more than one place can create uncertainty about what is actually available and who is responsible for fulfillment.

Customer risk

Listing overlap can lead to broken expectations, refunds, chargebacks, and damaged trust when fulfillment breaks down.

Operational drag

Manual review across CSV exports and listing records takes time. A cleaner duplicate check helps teams focus on the records that matter most.

How the review workflow works

  1. Upload or open exported listing data
  2. Compare sections, rows, and seats across records
  3. Group likely duplicates or conflicts
  4. Review flagged records manually
  5. Use findings to support trust, operations, or seller review

Best uses right now

  • Spot-checking one event before broader review
  • Testing listing exports for duplicate exposure
  • Supporting broker-side operations and trust workflows
  • Running pilot analyses before deeper automation

What this is

A practical duplicate review workflow that helps surface listing overlap and event-level issues sooner.

What this is not

Not a magical enforcement engine that vaporizes every listing problem in existence. It is a focused broker tool for identifying records that deserve attention.
