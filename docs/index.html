What does Ticket VeriGuard do?

Ticket VeriGuard monitors ticket listings across marketplaces to detect duplicate exposure and behavioral risk patterns. It provides visibility where existing inventory tools assume cooperation.

What it monitors

  • Listings across connected marketplaces
  • Seat, row, and section duplication
  • Repeated exposure patterns linked to disputes

How it works behind the scenes

  1. Scan listings across marketplaces
  2. Detect duplicate exposure and conflicts
  3. Flag high-risk patterns and sellers
  4. Present clear, human-readable signals
