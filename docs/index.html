What is Ticket VeriGuard?

Ticket VeriGuard keeps your ticket listings in sync across the internet. When one ticket sells, we instantly update all your other listings.

How to make it work

  • Connect your marketplaces or point of sale
  • Watch sales update in real time
  • Stop duplicate sales automatically

Behind the scenes

  1. We detect a sale event
  2. We lock that seat
  3. We notify your connected sites instantly
  4. They update their listings within seconds
