SeatGeek vs TickPick

Both platforms provide buyer protections and extensive inventories, but they differ in fees, user experience, and how they list tickets. Key Differences

  • Fees: StubHub typically charges higher fees than TickPick, which can significantly increase the total cost of your tickets.
  • Seller Ratings and Reviews: StubHub has a more established system for seller ratings and reviews, while TickPick’s system is less developed.
  • Marketplace Policies: Both platforms have different policies regarding guarantees, refunds, and buyer protection, so it’s important to review these before making a purchase.
  • Seat Details and Listings: The availability of tickets and the accuracy of seat details can vary between the two platforms, so it’s important to compare listings carefully.

  • Fees: StubHub typically charges higher fees compared to TickPick, which prides itself on being a "no-fee" marketplace. This means that the price you see on TickPick is the price you pay, while StubHub's prices may increase at checkout due to additional fees.
  • Seller Policies: Both platforms have different policies for sellers, which can affect the availability and pricing of tickets. It's important to review these policies before making a purchase

How to identify safe resale tickets

To identify safe resale tickets, consider the following tips:

  • Buy from reputable marketplaces with buyer protection policies.
  • Check the seller’s ratings and reviews to ensure they have a good track record.
  • Verify the exact location and number of tickets you are purchasing.
  • Read any additional information the seller provides about the tickets or how they will be delivered.
  • Compare the total cost including all fees to ensure you are getting a fair deal.

Marketplace policies and buyer protection

Each marketplace may handle guarantees, refunds, transfers, and delivery timing differently. Before buying, review the marketplace’s buyer protection language and make sure you understand what happens if an event is canceled, postponed, or if tickets are not delivered in time. This can help you make an informed decision when comparing listings and ensure that you are protected in case of any issues with your purchase.
