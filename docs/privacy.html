Privacy Policy

Ticket VeriGuard is designed to stay lightweight, simple, and fan-focused.

Saved ticket listings

Ticket listings you save may be stored locally in your browser using LocalStorage. This allows the site to remember your saved comparisons while you use Ticket VeriGuard.

Outbound marketplace links

When you click outbound marketplace links, Ticket VeriGuard may log basic information such as the ticket marketplace, event name, and outbound destination URL. This helps improve the site and understand which marketplaces users visit most often.

Analytics

Ticket VeriGuard uses Google Analytics to understand general site usage and traffic patterns.

Email communication

If you contact Ticket VeriGuard by email, your email address may be used to respond to your message.

Third-party marketplaces

Ticket VeriGuard links to third-party ticket marketplaces including StubHub, SeatGeek, Ticketmaster, Vivid Seats, and others. These sites have their own policies, pricing, and privacy practices.

No direct ticket sales

Ticket VeriGuard does not directly sell tickets, process payments, or manage ticket fulfillment.
