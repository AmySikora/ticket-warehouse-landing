StubHub vs SeatGeek

StubHub and SeatGeek are two of the largest ticket resale marketplaces, offering fans a wide range of options for concerts, sports events, theater performances, and more. Both platforms provide buyer protections and extensive inventories, but they differ in fees, user experience, and how they list tickets. Comparing listings across both marketplaces can help you find better deals and avoid duplicate listings before purchasing.

Ticket Selection

Both StubHub and SeatGeek offer extensive resale inventory. In many cases, the same ticket may appear on multiple marketplaces because sellers distribute listings through brokerage networks.

This can create situations where identical seats appear on more than one site at different prices.

Buyer Protection

Both marketplaces provide buyer guarantees designed to protect customers if tickets are invalid, not delivered, or unavailable. Policies can change over time, so buyers should review current terms directly on each marketplace before purchasing.

User Experience

SeatGeek is known for its Deal Score system and simplified shopping experience. StubHub offers one of the largest resale inventories and is widely recognized among event attendees.

Preferences often come down to pricing, event availability, and personal experience.

Which Marketplace Is Better?

There is no universal winner. The best option depends on the specific event, available inventory, fees, and seat location. The same ticket may cost less on one marketplace while being listed higher on another.
