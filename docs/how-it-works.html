How Ticket VeriGuard helps you shop smarter

Ticket VeriGuard helps you search ticket sites, save your favorite listings, compare prices and seats, and check for possible duplicate seats before you click out to buy.

The flow

1

Search ticket sites

Start with an artist, team, or event. Ticket VeriGuard helps you open searches across major ticket sites from one place so you can move faster.

2

Save listings worth tracking

As you browse, save the listings you actually care about, including price, section, row, seat details, ticket site, and link.

3

Compare your options

Review your saved listings side by side to spot price differences, seat details, and possible overlaps.

Why this helps

  • Ticket VeriGuard does not sell tickets directly
  • You still buy from the ticket site you choose
  • Prices and availability can change quickly
  • A duplicate-seat flag means “double-check this,” not “this is definitely bad”

What to know

  • Ticket VeriGuard does not sell tickets directly
  • Listings still live on the ticket site you choose
  • Availability and pricing can change at any time
  • A duplicate flag is a helpful signal, not a guarantee
