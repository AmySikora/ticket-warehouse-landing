Marketing & Go‑To‑Market Plan

3–6 month plan to attract early adopters, partners, and potential investors.

Executive Overview

Ticket Warehouse solves duplicate ticket sales with a vendor‑agnostic synchronization hub. This plan outlines how we’ll generate awareness, engagement, and conversions.

Target Segments

  • Independent Brokers: credibility + protection from oversells
  • Venues & Promoters: unified oversight and automation
  • Marketplaces: standardized, simple API integrations

Positioning

One Ticket. One Sale. One Source of Truth. — reliable, transparent, tech‑forward.

Phases & Tactics

  1. Awareness (M1–2): site + story post + demo video + outreach
  2. Engagement (M3–4): beta access, feedback sessions, technical blog
  3. Conversion (M5–6): case studies, paid tiers, investor pitches

Pricing

Starter $49/mo · Pro $199/mo · Enterprise Custom

Key Metrics

  • Beta sign‑ups, integrations, duplicate sales prevented
  • Conversion to paid, retention, site traffic

Funding

Seed goal: $25k for infra, outreach, legal, and dev support.
